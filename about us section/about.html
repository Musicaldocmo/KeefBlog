About Us

Keef Tour And Travels is dedicated to providing unforgettable travel experiences in Kashmir. Based in Srinagar, we specialize in bespoke tours that showcase the beauty of the region while supporting local communities.

Our services include trekking adventures, cultural tours, and luxurious stays tailored to meet the unique needs of every traveler.

We are passionate about delivering exceptional customer service, ensuring that each journey with us is filled with comfort, discovery, and adventure.
