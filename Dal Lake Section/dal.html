Explore the Beauty of Dal Lake

Dal Lake is the jewel of Srinagar, offering scenic views, houseboats, Mughal Gardens, and a glimpse into the local culture.

More About Dal Lake

Dal Lake is not just a lake, but a cultural hub. It has been an important part of Kashmiri life for centuries, offering a glimpse into the local way of life with its houseboats, Shikara rides, and floating gardens.

The surrounding Mughal Gardens, built in the 16th century, are a testament to the area's historical significance. Visitors can enjoy the peaceful serenity, explore the nearby markets, and experience the rich cultural heritage of Kashmir.

Don't miss the chance to witness the captivating sunrise and sunset views over Dal Lake, reflecting the majestic Himalayan peaks in its tranquil waters.

The Iconic Mughal Gardens

Shalimar Bagh

Shalimar Bagh is one of the largest Mughal gardens in Srinagar, known for its beautifully landscaped greenery, fountains, and architecture.

Nishat Bagh

Famous for its terraces and stunning view of Dal Lake, Nishat Bagh offers an exceptional experience of Mughal-era gardening.

Tulip Garden

Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden is the largest in Asia, and it blooms with thousands of tulips in different colors every spring.

Pari Mahal

Perched on Zabarwan mountain, Pari Mahal, meaning 'The Palace of Fairies,' offers a stunning view of Dal Lake.

Chashme Shahi

Chashme Shahi, meaning 'The Royal Spring,' is a small, yet beautiful Mughal garden with natural water springs.

Explore Boulevard Road

The Boulevard Road along Dal Lake is perfect for enjoying long walks, delicious barbeques, and local street food. It’s a vibrant area full of restaurants, cafés, and street vendors.

Where to Eat

Barbeque Nation

Delicious grilled food with a stunning view of Dal Lake.

Shawarma Street

Famous for its Middle Eastern delicacies, especially shawarmas.

Grill House

Local barbecue dishes with a Mughlai twist.

Shopping in Srinagar

Discover the local markets in Srinagar, famous for Kashmiri handicrafts, pashmina shawls, carpets, and much more. A must-visit is the Lal Chowk area for an authentic shopping experience.

Lal Chowk

The heart of Srinagar’s shopping scene, offering traditional Kashmiri crafts, clothes, and food.

Polo View Market

Known for its exclusive pashmina shawls and local handicrafts.

Residency Road

Popular for dry fruits, spices, and Kashmiri tea (Kehwa).

Must-Try Eating Places

Savor the authentic flavors of Kashmir at these popular dining spots.

Ahdoos

A historic restaurant known for its authentic Kashmiri dishes.

Shamyana Restaurant

Offers a beautiful view of Dal Lake along with delicious food.

Krishna Vaishno Dhaba

Popular for its vegetarian options and traditional meals.

Activities Around Dal Lake

Dal Lake offers numerous activities, from houseboat stays to Shikara rides, birdwatching, and exploring the floating gardens.

Shikara Ride

Experience the beauty of Dal Lake with a peaceful Shikara ride across the water.

Houseboat Stay

Spend a night in one of the famous houseboats, a unique experience offering luxury and tranquility.

Floating Gardens

Explore the unique floating gardens on Dal Lake like Nehru-Park,Zabarwan-Park, known for their beauty and the local farming techniques.
